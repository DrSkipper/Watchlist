
New watchlist weapon paradigm:

Note: Once at max level with weapon, continued pickups just replenish timer.
Idea: Kills pause timer.

Spreadshot: 3 levels (2 shot, 3 shot, 5 shot), very common, short effect duration
Bomb:       3 levels (increasing size of explosion), common, short effect duration
Bounce:     2 levels (2 bounces, 4 bounces), common, medium effect duration
Laser:      1 level  (applies laser effect to weapon), uncommon, long effect duration

Drop weights:
very common: 5
common: 3
uncommon: 1

Effect durations:
short: 5s
medium: 9s
long: 12s
